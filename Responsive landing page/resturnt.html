Living Well Begins With Eating Well.

Hotel Vrushabs in Shedbal is a popular dining spot known for its delicious North Karnataka and Maharashtrian cuisine. Offering a warm and family-friendly ambiance, the restaurant serves local specialties like Jolad Roti, spicy curries, and traditional vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes. With affordable pricing and generous portions, it attracts both locals and travelers looking for an authentic taste of the region. Conveniently located in Shedbal, it is a great place to enjoy a hearty meal with a touch of local hospitality.
